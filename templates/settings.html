About Smart Calendar Agent AI

Smart Calendar Agent AI is a local AI productivity system built with Flask, Google Calendar API, Ollama, Bootstrap and Chart.js. It analyzes calendar load, protects focus time, detects burnout risk and manages calendar actions through a Copilot-style assistant.
